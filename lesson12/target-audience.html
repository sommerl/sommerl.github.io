Target Audience

This website is for anyone who is looking for one of the greatest and easiest ways to visit all the amazing and beautiful destinations found on the island of Conzumel. It also provides a way for those who have a trip planed, are interested in planning a trip, or are already on the Island of Cozumel to make reservations for scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs while they are on the island. It helps explain the different rental locations, different catering options available, and help those who want to have all the details of their trip planned out. It also helps to show and reflect the fun that takes place on the Island of Cozumel.


Persona 1

Persona: Sarah Call

Sarah is a 45 year old women. Her and her husband have six kids. They enjoy traveling together, spending time in the outdoors, and playing and watching sports. Sarah works from home for the Blue Sea Cruise Ship Company, and during her other free time is a stay at home mom.


Scenarios

  • Sarah has been assigned by her manager at Blue Sea Cruise Ship to find a company located on the island Cozumel, where the company can rent out scooters for each of their employees they are taking on a company trip.
  • Sarah and her husband want to plan a trip to the island Conzumel located off the coast of Mexico. They talked about things they wanted to do as a family, and her son John wants to rent scooters to go sight seeing around the island.


Persona 2

Persona: Sam Chavez

Sam Chavez, 18, just graduated from high school. He is the oldest out of his 5 siblings. He enjoys playing soccer, spending time with his family and friends, and mountain biking. He also loves to spend time and play with his dog, Tucker. Sam is starting his first Summer of summer sales.


Scenarios

  • Sam's summer sale's company has planned a trip to take everyone in their company to the island of Conzumel to kick of the start to their selling season. In the instruction packet for the trip he is repsonsible to find a way for himself to easily get around on the island. They recommened renting a scooter to make for easily traveling to different locations on the island.
  • Sam's parents are taking him on a senior trip to Conzumel, an island located off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. Something he wants to do is for each of them to rent scooters to easily get to different destinations on the island without having to be worried about getting caught in traffic.
