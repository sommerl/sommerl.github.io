Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to provide a way for those who have a tripped planed, are interested in planning a trip, or already on the Island of Cozumel to make reservations for scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs while they are on the island. It helps explain the different rental locations, different catering options available, and help those who want to have all the details of their trip planned out. It also helps to show and reflect the fun that takes place on the Island of Cozumel. These rentals will help individuals to be able to visit the touristy western part of the Island, with easy access to different areas when they rent with us at Scoots.
